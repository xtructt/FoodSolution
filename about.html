About Us Our Mission

Our mission as well as our idea is creating the home-made food supply "platform" for busy people and connecting between homecooker and people who need it. Through our easy-to-navigate website, you can effortlessly find and order whatever you feel like, wherever you are, at the touch of a button. Whether it’s beating the queue at your favourite local cafe, providing for a conference, having lunch delivered at work or opting for a quick and easy dinner, Food Solutions has you covered.
